Final Build

TRIGGERS

Users type in their information on the input box.
Users click on the "I am not a robot" button.
Users click on the "Dismiss" button.

RULES

The microinteraction can only be seen when users click the button.
Users can double click on the images on the popup gallery to un-select.

FEEDBACK

When users click the button, the button's background change from black to white color and the text changes from white to black color.
Then, a pop-up box shows up in the center, placing on top of the content box.
Users hover on each image, the image turns to grayscale.
Users click to select, and an arrow appears on selected image.
Users click "Dismiss" button, the popup box disappears.

LOOPS

There are no loops in this microinteraction.
